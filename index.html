What is the correct way to cook rice?

1. Rinse the rice
2. Use the correct ration of water to rice. For one cup of rice add two parts water.
3. Bring water to a boil, once the water is boiling add a pinch of salt.
4. Add rice and reduce the heat to a simmer, make sure to cover with a right fitting lid.
5. Do no remove the lid! Allow the rice to cook for 18-20 mins without interruption.
6. Turn off the heat and let the rice rest for an extra 10 mins.
7. Fluff with a fork.

How can you tell what temperature a steak is by touch?

1. Raw: Feel the palm of your hand, just below your thumb. It’s a little soft and fleshy. This is what raw steak should feel like when you touch it.
2. Rare: Now bring your thumb to your pointer finger, and touch that same part of your palm again. Did you notice how it’s slightly firmer? A rare steak should feel like this when you touch it.
3. Med-rare: Touch your thumb to your middle finger. Notice how that section of your palm tenses up a little more? This is what a medium-­rare steak feels like.
4. Medium: Move your thumb to your ring finger. Now the palm may feel slightly flexed. This level of firmness means your steak is cooked medium.
5. Well-done: Now touch your thumb to your pinky. That section of your palm will be fairly firm. This is what a well-­done steak feels like.

What is the difference between baking powder and baking soda?

Baking soda is sodium bicarbonate, which requires an acid and a liquid to become activated and help baked goods rise. Baking powder includes sodium bicarbonate, as well as an acid. It only needs a liquid to become activated.
